Refund Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

We want you to be happy with FloatDeck Pro. If it isn’t right for you, we offer a straightforward refund.

14-day money-back guarantee

You may request a full refund of your FloatDeck Pro purchase within 14 days of the purchase date, no questions asked.

How to request a refund

  • Email ceo@tapdot.org from the address you used to buy, with your order number (from your purchase receipt).
  • We’ll process the refund through Lemon Squeezy (our Merchant of Record). Refunds are returned to your original payment method, typically within 5–10 business days depending on your bank.
  • On refund, your FloatDeck Pro license is deactivated; the free version (all core actions + 1 slot) keeps working.

Notes

  • FloatDeck Pro is a one-time digital purchase. Because every action is free to use without paying, you can fully evaluate FloatDeck before buying.
  • Requests made after 14 days may still be considered at our discretion.
  • Lemon Squeezy may also process refunds in line with their own policies.
